5 · Governance, restated

  • No individual scores leave the user's account; this report is generated from de-identified aggregates only.
  • Minimum reporting cell of 10 — any slice smaller than that is suppressed.
  • Employers, foremen, and crews never see individual or site-level data. Stated in-product.
  • Associations shown are self-reported and observational; causal claims require the comparison-group design outlined in the Measurement & Evaluation Plan.
